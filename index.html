About Us

At Moana's Kitchen, we bring the rich, flavorful spices and traditions of Indian cuisine to Calgary. We offer a wide range of dishes that capture the essence of Indian culture, prepared with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our chef's recipes are based on years of culinary experience and passion for authentic flavors.
